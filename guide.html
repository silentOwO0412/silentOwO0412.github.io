Minecraft

📝 Basic Gameplay

In Survival mode, you must gather resources (like wood and stone), craft tools, and build a shelter during the day to defend against monsters that spawn at night. If you prefer unlimited resources and complete freedom, choose Creative mode to build massive structures without limits.

⌨️ Basic Keybinds

KeyAction
W, A, S, DMovement
SpacebarJump
Left ClickBreak Block / Attack
Right ClickPlace Block / Use Item
EOpen Inventory

League of Legends

📝 Basic Gameplay

This is a 5v5 objective-based team game. You must work with your team to defeat enemy minions, monsters, and champions across three lanes (Top, Mid, Bot) or the jungle to earn gold and purchase items. The ultimate goal is to push into the enemy base and destroy their Nexus.

⌨️ Basic Keybinds

KeyAction
Right ClickMove / Basic Attack
Q, W, E, RCast Champion Abilities
D, FSummoner Spells (e.g., Flash, Ignite)
BRecall to Base
